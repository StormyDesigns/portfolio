At Mayo Clinic, I design digital experiences that support patients throughout their healthcare journey, from understanding their health information to navigating care with confidence.

I partner closely with product, engineering, and research teams to identify patient needs, simplify complex medical information, and translate them into intuitive, accessible solutions across mobile and web platforms. My work focuses on improving how patients engage with their care, reducing confusion, and empowering them to make informed decisions.

While much of this work is not yet public, I've contributed to initiatives aimed at transforming the digital healthcare experience, bringing greater clarity, usability, and patient-centered thinking to traditionally complex systems.

🔒
